Stellar Burner: High-Speed ​​Spaceship Racing Challenge

Stellar Burner is a high-speed spaceship racing game where you pilot a spaceship through tight obstacles at breakneck speeds. The stellar burner focuses on instant reflexes and skillful dodging skills, where every millisecond of hesitation can lead to a collision.  As the game progresses, the speed of the spaceship increases, the obstacles become denser, and the safe distance becomes so fragile that it requires players to truly master each control operation.

Objectives & Missions

Main Objective: Complete 6 game zones, each with 5 stages of increasing difficulty.

Tough Challenges:

  • Fly through narrow gaps that are only wide enough for your spaceship.
  • Avoid moving obstacles like meteors and energy walls.

Unique Features

Instant respawn: No waiting; failure is just a moment before you rush back into battle.

Fast-paced action: Challenge yourself with fast-paced gameplay and compete for global rankings.

Unlock new ships: Each spacecraft has a different design, speed, and stats. The Scout ship has moderate speed, making it ideal for beginners. In contrast, the Phantom ship boasts high speed but is difficult to control, making it suitable for experts. Play is difficult to unlock.

How to play Stellar Burner game

Control the Spaceship

Use the left mouse to steer the ship in the desired direction. The later you go, the faster the ship will go, so you need to prioritize controlling the ship at a tight angle. Don't recklessly rush into a narrow gap if you are not proficient.

Keep a safe distance from obstacles

  • Even the smallest collision will make you explode!
  • Fly through a narrow V-shaped gap; keep a distance of 1-2mm from obstacles.
  • Obstacles move in a fixed pattern. Players remember 3-4 common movement patterns to avoid 0.5 seconds in advance.
  • Rotating the ship 45 degrees when flying through a circular gate will reduce the risk of collision by 30% compared to flying straight.

How to control the direction of the spaceship?

Lightly tap the screen to change the direction of the flight; do not hold for a long time (the game uses the "tap-to-turn" mechanism). Each tap is a 90° rotation.

Why do spaceships often crash into walls?

Due to flight inertia, the ship continues to move in the same direction after turning. You need to turn 1-2 seconds earlier than the obstacle.

How to get the most points?

Fly close to the wall/edge of the tunnel to get x2 bonus points and collect diamonds at the same time.

Is it possible to upgrade the ship?

There are many types of unlocked ships, each with different speeds and flexibility. You only unlock new ships when you pass certain stages and reach specific score milestones.

How to fly through the "spiral" screen?

To successfully navigate the spiral section, time your rotations precisely. Tap to rotate your ship each time the wall completes a 90-degree turn (1/4 rotation). The key is maintaining consistent speed while syncing your movements with the spiral's rhythm.
