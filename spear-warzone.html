Spear Warzone

Rating:3.7 (25 votes)
Played:4981 times
Developer:Azgames
Released:May 9, 2025
Platform:Browser (desktop, mobile, tablet)
Technology:HTML5
Classification:Casual Games

Spear Warzone is a 2D action game where players control a stickman warrior with a spear in hand, fighting against various enemies such as archers, swordsmen, javelin throwers, and mages. The game focuses on throwing spears at enemies while dodging their attacks. As you progress in the game, the number of enemies will increase, and they will become stronger, requiring players to have high accuracy and quick reflexes to defeat them.

How to play Spear Warzone

How to Control Stickman

Drag & Drop: Hold the mouse to aim the flight path, and release to throw the spear. Fixed throwing power and angle decide everything!

Move wisely: Although you can't run, you can dodge attacks by quickly dragging the mouse in the opposite direction immediately after throwing.

Take advantage of "blind spots": Stand off center to limit the enemy's vision.

How to Throw a Spear Accurately: "One Shot"

To take down an opponent with one shot, you need to aim at the opponent's head or chest to throw the spear. Thrown at a 30- to 45- degree angle, the spear will fly in an arc, fooling the shield. Throw straight horizontally (0 degrees) so the spear will fly quickly, avoiding the opponent's ability to dodge. When the archer raises the bow, you should throw the spear immediately.

Stickman Warrior Upgrades: From "Wood" to "Iron"

Prioritize survival upgrades, such as an armored helmet or a knight helmet.

  • Armored helmet (200 coins): Blocks 2 arrows to the head.
  • Knight helmet (500 coins): Blocks 3 hits, automatically recovers after 10 seconds.
  • Reflective Shield (800 coins): This ability creates a magic counterattack shield that disrupts the mage's mental processes.

Other Attack Upgrades

Green Spear (reach 30 points): Increases flying speed by 20% and easily hits moving targets.

Energy Ring (300 coins): Destroys all flying bullets within a 2m radius when activated.

Hidden trick: Buy a Healing Apple (100 coins), but only use it when under 30% health and surrounded.

How to Collect the Most Coins

  • Combo "x3": Kill three enemies in a row without missing to get 50% coins.
  • Mage: Gives 15 coins (highest); easy to kill with 1 shot to the head.
  • Archer: 10 coins, usually appears in groups; uses Green Spear to kill.
  • Event Hunting: Every 5 minutes, kill a golden enemy and get 100 coins!
  • Reuse spears: You can pick up spears stuck in walls or the ground again; don't waste them!

FAQs

How to defeat the final boss?

The boss has 3 weak points: head, heart, and back. Throw 3 accurate shots at these 3 spots to activate the x3 combo to defeat the Boss in 5 seconds!

Why buy the Reflective Shield before the Helm?

The shield blocks magic and missiles, while the helmet only protects against physical attacks. Prioritize the shield when facing multiple mages!

What is the most efficient method for evasion?

After throwing the spear, drag the mouse down immediately—the stickman will crouch, avoiding 90% of the flying bullets.

Who published Spear Warzone?

Conclusion

Spear Warzone is more than just a game; it's the art of spear throwing that requires composure and tactical vision. Become a legend of the arena, where every throw strikes the enemy with fear!
